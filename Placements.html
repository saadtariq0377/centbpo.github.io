RTO Partnership

Overview

Centaurus BPO acts as the liaison between Institutes, students, and Host Employers. We have extensive experience working with reputable RTOs across Australia, making us the ideal partner for your placement needs.

Benefits for RTOs

  • Relieve your burden of finding suitable placements.
  • Access to a diverse list of qualified workplaces.
  • Efficient, fast, and easy placement process.
  • Full student support to ensure a successful placement experience.

Key Features

  • Portal access for RTOs to view student data and documents.
  • Real-time updates and reporting on student placements.
  • Weekly reports for easy tracking of student progress.
  • Email copies and call recordings for record-keeping.

Student Placement

Introduction

Centaurus BPO offers expert student placement services for accredited courses in partnership with RTOs across Australia. We understand the importance of completing placement hours to obtain certification and aim to provide students with valuable, practical experiences to support their career journey.

Student Placement Process

Our student placement process is designed to ensure a seamless experience for both students and workplaces. Here's how it works:

  • Students introduce themselves and their preferences.
  • We provide a welcome pack via email.
  • Students can choose between their own workplace or request our assistance.
  • Workplace confirmation for students with existing workplaces.
  • Students requesting a workplace undergo our thorough application and interview process.
  • Book appointments for students to begin their placements.

Features for Students

  • Personalized support throughout the placement journey.
  • Matching students with the most suitable workplaces.
  • Easy access to documents and schedules via our portal.
  • Real-time updates and reporting on placement status and progress.

Workplace Coordination

Coordination Services

Centaurus BPO connects workplaces with talented and motivated students seeking practical experience. Our services for workplaces include:

  • Matching workplaces with students who fit their requirements and programs.
  • Providing schedules and qualifications of potential students for review.
  • Assisting with necessary documentation and background checks.
  • Offering ongoing support to workplaces during the placement period.

Benefits for Workplaces

  • Access to a pool of qualified and talented students eager to contribute to workplace operations.
  • Opportunity to evaluate students during their placements for potential future employment.
  • Efficient and personalized matching process for optimal workplace-student pairings.
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate workplace needs and preferences.

Features for Workplaces

  • Access to a wide range of students with diverse skills and qualifications.
  • Ongoing support and communication throughout the placement period.
  • Potential employment opportunities based on student performance.
  • Easy access to students' documentation and placement details.
