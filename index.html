About Us

Centaurus BPO is a trusted placement agency that specializes in connecting institutes, students, and host employers. With a team of experienced professionals and strong partnerships with reputable RTOs (Registered Training Organizations), we ensure a seamless placement process that benefits all parties involved.We specialize in student placement services, providing a seamless process for students to find suitable workplace opportunities. Our comprehensive support includes understanding student availability, providing regular updates, and offering full access to agreement documents, schedules, and placement details.

At Centaurus BPO, we pride ourselves on our efficiency, reliability, and dedication to delivering exceptional service. Our team works closely with RTOs to understand their unique needs and requirements, ensuring that we match students with the most suitable workplaces. We believe in the power of collaboration and aim to foster strong relationships between institutes and host employers, ultimately benefiting students and the industries they enter.

What We Do

At Centaurus BPO, we offer a range of services to facilitate successful student placements and support their transition into the workforce. Our Services Include:

Student Placement Assistance

We specialize in providing student placement services for accredited courses offered by RTOs across Australia. We match students with suitable placement opportunities based on their schedules, course requirements, and desired learning outcomes.

Paid Jobs Support

After students complete their courses, we assist them in finding paid job opportunities in their respective fields. Our extensive network and industry connections enable us to connect students with potential employers and help them kickstart their careers.

Document Preparation

We guide students in preparing necessary documents required for their placements, such as prerequisite certifications, police checks, and COVID-19 vaccination records. Our team ensures that students are well-prepared to meet workplace requirements.

Workplace Coordination

We liaise with workplaces within the student's preferred locations to secure suitable placement opportunities. Our aim is to minimize travel time and ensure a convenient and eligible placement experience for students per their course requirement and expertise.

Student Support

We provide comprehensive support to students throughout their placement journey. Our dedicated coordinators are available to address their queries, offer guidance, and ensure a smooth and enriching placement experience throught this journey.

RTO Partnership

We collaborate with Registered Training Organizations (RTOs) across Australia, serving as a reliable liaison between the institutes, students, and host employers. Our expertise and experience in working with reputable RTOs make us a trusted partner in student placement services.
